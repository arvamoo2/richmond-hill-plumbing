What We Do

Our Core Services

Six specialized plumbing disciplines. One unwavering standard of quality.

24/7 Emergency Dispatch

Burst pipes, sewage backups, flooding, and gas-line leaks don't wait for business hours — and neither do we. Our emergency fleet is GPS-tracked and rolling within minutes of your call, every hour of every day.

Pro Tip: Turn off your main water shutoff valve immediately to minimize damage while you wait for us.

Drain Cleaning & Clogs

Kitchen sinks, floor drains, main sewer lines — we clear them all. Our truck-mounted hydro-jetting and camera-fed snake technology eliminates blockages completely, not just temporarily.

Pro Tip: We use camera-fed snake tech for 100% clearing — you'll see the before and after on screen.

Leak Detection & Repair

Hidden leaks can cause thousands in damage before you notice them. We use acoustic listening devices and thermal imaging to pinpoint leaks inside walls, under slabs, and behind fixtures — with zero guesswork.

Pro Tip: An unexplained spike in your water bill almost always means a hidden leak. Call for a free assessment.

Water Heater Service

Tank and tankless installations, repairs, and replacements. We carry Rheem, AO Smith, Navien, and Rinnai inventory on our trucks for same-day hot water restoration. Full warranty on every install.

Pro Tip: Tankless systems save up to 30% on energy. Ask us about the Navien NPE-240A for York Region homes.

Sump Pump Systems

Protect your basement from York Region's heavy rains and spring thaws. We install, replace, and maintain primary and battery-backup sump pump systems with check-valve protection and alarm monitoring.

Pro Tip: Always install a battery backup — power outages during storms are exactly when your sump pump fails.

Sinks, Faucets & Fixtures

From kitchen sink replacements to bathroom faucet upgrades and toilet installations. We work with Kohler, Moen, Delta, and all major brands. Precision installation with leak-free guarantees.

Pro Tip: A dripping faucet wastes up to 3,000 gallons/year. We'll fix or upgrade it in a single visit.
